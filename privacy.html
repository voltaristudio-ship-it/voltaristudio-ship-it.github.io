Privacy Policy

Neonaut · Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Voltari Studio (we, us) handles information in connection with the mobile game Neonaut (the Game).

Information We Collect

Neonaut does not collect or store your personal information on our own servers. Your game progress - such as high scores, gold and unlocked ships - is saved locally on your device. If you sign in to Google Play Games, this progress may also be synced to your Google account using Play Games Saved Games.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may process limited data: Google AdMob (advertising), which may use your device advertising identifier to show ads; and Google Play Games Services (leaderboards and cloud save). These services have their own privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Advertising Identifier

Ads in the Game are provided by Google AdMob, which may use a resettable advertising identifier. You can reset this identifier or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings.

Children

Neonaut is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at voltari.studio@gmail.com.
